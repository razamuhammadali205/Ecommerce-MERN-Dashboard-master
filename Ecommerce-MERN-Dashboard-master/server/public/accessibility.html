Accessibility Statement

At SkinCeuticels Skincare, we are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including those with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive online experience that meets or exceeds WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards.

Our Commitment to Digital Accessibility

SkinCeuticels Skincare is dedicated to making our website accessible to everyone. We believe that all individuals should have equal access to information, products, and services regardless of ability. We continuously work to improve the usability and accessibility of our digital properties in accordance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

Our accessibility efforts are guided by the following principles:

  • Perceivable: Information and user interface components must be presentable to users in ways they can perceive.
  • Operable: User interface components and navigation must be operable by all users.
  • Understandable: Information and the operation of the user interface must be understandable.
  • Robust: Content must be robust enough to be interpreted reliably by a wide variety of user agents, including assistive technologies.

Ongoing Accessibility Improvements

We regularly review and enhance our website to ensure compliance with accessibility best practices. Some of the measures we have implemented include:

Keyboard Navigation

All functionality is available via keyboard. Users can navigate through our site using Tab, Shift+Tab, arrow keys, and Enter without a mouse.

Screen Reader Compatibility

Our site is compatible with popular screen readers including NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver, and TalkBack. Proper ARIA labels and landmarks are used throughout.

Alternative Text for Images

All meaningful images include descriptive alt text. Decorative images are properly marked to be ignored by screen readers.

Color Contrast & Readability

Text meets minimum contrast ratios of 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text. Font sizes are scalable up to 200% without loss of content or functionality.

Responsive Design

Our website adapts seamlessly to different screen sizes and orientations, ensuring usability on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.

Clear Language & Structure

Content is organized with proper heading hierarchy (H1-H6), descriptive link text, and clear, concise language suitable for all reading levels.

WCAG 2.1 Level AA Compliance Features

  • Success Criterion 1.1.1: Non-text Content – All non-text content has text alternatives
  • Success Criterion 1.3.1: Info and Relationships – Information, structure, and relationships are determinable
  • Success Criterion 1.4.1: Use of Color – Color is not used as the only visual means of conveying information
  • Success Criterion 1.4.3: Contrast (Minimum) – Text has sufficient contrast against background
  • Success Criterion 1.4.4: Resize Text – Text can be resized up to 200% without loss of content
  • Success Criterion 2.1.1: Keyboard – All functionality is available from a keyboard
  • Success Criterion 2.4.1: Bypass Blocks – Mechanism to bypass blocks of content
  • Success Criterion 2.4.4: Link Purpose – Purpose of each link can be determined from link text
  • Success Criterion 2.4.7: Focus Visible – Keyboard focus indicator is visible
  • Success Criterion 3.1.1: Language of Page – Default human language is specified
  • Success Criterion 3.3.2: Labels or Instructions – Labels are provided for input fields
  • Success Criterion 4.1.2: Name, Role, Value – User interface components have name, role, and value

Third-Party Content

Some areas of our website may include third-party content or plugins (such as social media widgets, videos, or payment processors). While we strive to select accessible partners, we cannot guarantee the accessibility of all third-party content. We actively work with our vendors to improve accessibility where possible.

Assistive Technology Compatibility

We test our website regularly with the following assistive technologies:

  • Screen readers: NVDA (Windows), JAWS (Windows), VoiceOver (macOS/iOS), TalkBack (Android)
  • Browser zoom and high-contrast modes
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • Speech recognition software
  • Braille displays

Accessibility in Our Physical Locations

Our commitment to accessibility extends beyond digital properties. All SkinCeuticels flagship stores and partner clinics comply with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and provide:

  • Wheelchair-accessible entrances and pathways
  • Accessible restrooms
  • Braille signage
  • Staff training in disability awareness and assistance
  • Alternative formats for printed materials upon request

Continuous Improvement Process

Accessibility is an ongoing journey. We have established the following processes to maintain and improve accessibility:

  • Monthly accessibility audits using automated tools and manual testing
  • Quarterly user testing with individuals with disabilities
  • Annual third-party accessibility evaluation
  • Regular training for web development and content teams
  • Accessibility considerations in all new feature development
  • Monitoring of new WCAG guidelines and emerging best practices

Legal Compliance

This accessibility statement is in accordance with:

  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA
  • Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act
  • Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) Title III
  • Twenty-First Century Communications and Video Accessibility Act
  • European Standard EN 301 549
