About SkinCeuticels

Discover the science, innovation, and commitment behind our advanced skincare solutions designed to prevent, protect, and correct skin concerns for radiant, healthy skin.

Our Story and Legacy

SkinCeuticels Skincare was founded with a mission to revolutionize skincare through cutting-edge science and clinical expertise. Established in 1997 by a team of dermatologists and scientists, our brand has grown to become a global leader in medical aesthetic skincare, trusted by over 10,000 clinics and millions of users worldwide.

Our journey began with groundbreaking research on vitamin C stabilization, leading to patented technologies that set new standards in antioxidant protection. Today, SkinCeuticels continues to innovate, blending natural ingredients with advanced formulations to address a wide range of skin concerns, from aging and acne to hyperpigmentation and sensitivity.

We believe skincare is more than a routine—it's an investment in your health. That's why every product is rigorously tested in clinical trials, ensuring efficacy, safety, and visible results. Our commitment to transparency means we share detailed ingredient lists, research findings, and user testimonials openly.

Over the years, SkinCeuticels has expanded its portfolio to include serums, moisturizers, sunscreens, and professional treatments. We've partnered with leading dermatologists, estheticians, and research institutions to stay at the forefront of skincare science. Our products are formulated without parabens, sulfates, or artificial fragrances, prioritizing skin compatibility and environmental responsibility.

As we look to the future, SkinCeuticels is dedicated to sustainability, with initiatives like recyclable packaging, ethical sourcing, and carbon-neutral manufacturing. We also support global skin health programs, donating a portion of proceeds to dermatological research and access to care in underserved communities.

Our Mission

At SkinCeuticels, our mission is to empower individuals with science-backed skincare that delivers real results. We aim to prevent future damage, protect healthy skin, and correct existing concerns through innovative, integrated solutions. Our philosophy centers on personalization, ensuring every routine is tailored to unique skin needs for optimal health and beauty.

We strive to make advanced skincare accessible, educating consumers on skin science while providing high-quality products at fair prices. Our commitment extends to ethical practices, from ingredient sourcing to manufacturing, always prioritizing people and the planet.

Our Science

SkinCeuticels is built on a foundation of rigorous scientific research. Our labs employ state-of-the-art technology to develop formulations that penetrate deeply and work synergistically with the skin's natural processes. Key innovations include our patented C E Ferulic serum, which provides 8x environmental protection against free radicals.

We conduct extensive clinical studies, often in collaboration with universities and medical centers, to validate product efficacy. For example, our Triple Lipid Restore cream has been shown to improve skin barrier function by 60% in just four weeks. We also invest in emerging technologies like peptide science and microbiome research to stay ahead of industry trends.

Transparency is key—each product page includes detailed ingredient breakdowns, pH levels, and stability data. Our scientists publish in peer-reviewed journals, contributing to the broader field of dermatology.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

SkinCeuticels is committed to environmental stewardship. We use sustainably sourced ingredients, such as organic botanicals and responsibly harvested marine extracts. Our packaging is 100% recyclable, and we're transitioning to refillable systems to reduce waste.

We partner with organizations like the Rainforest Alliance and Ocean Conservancy to support conservation efforts. Internally, our facilities run on renewable energy, and we've achieved zero-waste status in manufacturing. Every purchase contributes to our goal of planting 1 million trees by 2030.

Our History Timeline

1997

Founding and Vitamin C Breakthrough

SkinCeuticels is founded by Dr. Sheldon Pinnell, a pioneering dermatologist. The brand launches with C E Ferulic, a stabilized vitamin C serum that revolutionizes antioxidant skincare, offering unprecedented protection against UV and pollution damage.

This innovation stems from years of research at Duke University, where Dr. Pinnell discovers how to stabilize L-ascorbic acid for topical application. The product quickly gains recognition in medical circles for its ability to reduce fine lines and improve skin texture.

2005

Expansion into Professional Treatments

We introduce our first in-office treatments, partnering with clinics for integrated skincare protocols. This marks the beginning of our dual approach: at-home products complemented by professional services for enhanced results.

New products like Phloretin CF are launched, targeting discoloration and firmness. Our network grows to 5,000 clinics, establishing SkinCeuticels as a staple in dermatological practices worldwide.

2015

Sustainability Initiatives Launch

SkinCeuticels commits to sustainability with eco-friendly packaging and ethical sourcing. We release our first microbiome-friendly products, recognizing the importance of skin's natural bacteria in overall health.

Global expansion reaches Asia and Europe, with localized formulations for different climates and skin types. Awards from beauty industry bodies affirm our leadership in clean, effective skincare.

2023

Digital Innovation and Personalization

Launch of AI-powered routine builder and virtual consultations. New peptide serums address advanced anti-aging needs. We achieve B-Corp certification, highlighting our dedication to social and environmental performance.

Partnerships with influencers and experts amplify our educational content, reaching millions through social media and webinars on skin science.
