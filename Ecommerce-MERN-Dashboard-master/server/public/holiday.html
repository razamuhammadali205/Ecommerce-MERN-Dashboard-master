Expert Gifting Tips

Not sure where to start? Our skincare specialists share professional insights to help you choose the perfect gift for every skin type and concern.

1

Know Their Skin Type

Dry skin benefits from lipid-replenishing creams like Triple Lipid Restore. Oily or acne-prone skin thrives with lightweight serums like Phloretin CF. Combination skin? Layer a hydrating serum under a matte moisturizer.

2

Focus on Concerns

Target specific issues: C E Ferulic for environmental protection, H.A. Intensifier for plumpness, Retinol 0.3 for texture and lines. Pairing actives with antioxidants maximizes efficacy and minimizes irritation.

3

Layering is Key

Teach them the correct order: Cleanse → Serum (vitamin C in AM, retinol in PM) → Moisturizer → SPF (day). Our gift sets include step-by-step regimen cards for easy adoption.

4

Start Slow with Actives

Retinol and high-potency vitamin C should be introduced gradually. Recommend using 2–3 times per week initially, then building tolerance. Always follow with broad-spectrum SPF in the morning.
